## 2. Executive Guidance & Context Layer
<executive_brief_block>
### A. Long Comprehensive Summary
[Target:: LocalHealthServices | Milestone:: Directory | Action:: List] This document provides a detailed directory of public health services and facilities primarily managed by the Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai (MCGM) and the Maharashtra state government within Mumbai, as well as specific service schedules for health posts in Bhiwandi and under the Healthy Cities Program. It lists various types of healthcare facilities including medical college hospitals, specialty hospitals, peripheral hospitals, maternity homes, dispensaries, and health posts. For many of these facilities, specific names and addresses are provided, particularly for those located in Mumbai's PN Ward. The document also outlines the specific days and services offered at several health posts in Bhiwandi, such as HB testing, Antenatal Care (ANC) check-ups, and immunization. Similarly, it details the schedules for these services at Health Post 1, Health Post 2, and Rathodi Health Post as part of the Healthy Cities Program. The information is based on source documents referencing the MCGM public health department's 2015-16 Health Profile.
