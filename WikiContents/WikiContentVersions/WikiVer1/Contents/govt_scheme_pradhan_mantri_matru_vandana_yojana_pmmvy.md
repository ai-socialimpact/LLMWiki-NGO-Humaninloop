### A. Long Comprehensive Summary
[Target:: pmmvy | Milestone:: scheme_overview | Action:: summary] The Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY) is a government maternity benefit scheme designed to provide cash compensation to expecting and lactating mothers. The primary objective is to allow mothers to take adequate rest before and after delivery without facing financial hardship due to wage loss. For the first live birth, a total of Rs 5,000 is provided in two installments: Rs 3,000 after at least one antenatal check-up within six months of pregnancy, and Rs 2,000 after the child's birth is registered and the initial 14-week vaccination cycle is complete. The scheme was updated effective April 1, 2022, to include a benefit for a second child, but only if the child is a girl; in this case, Rs 6,000 is given in a single installment after birth registration and completion of the 14-week vaccination cycle. Eligibility is restricted to women who meet specific criteria, such as having a family income under Rs 8 lakh per year, belonging to SC/ST communities, or holding an NREGA job card, among others. Government employees receiving maternity benefits are not eligible. Applications are processed through Anganwadi Centres (AWC) or health facilities, and the benefit is transferred directly to the beneficiary's Aadhaar-linked bank account via Direct Beneficiary Transfer (DBT).
